## What Is ToolScale, Exactly?

ToolScale operates on a shared access model — kind of like a co-working space for software. You subscribe, you get a member dashboard, and from there you can access everything from VEO3 (Google's top-tier AI video generator) to ElevenLabs, Pipiads, Canva Pro, HeyGen, Semrush, Figma, and a bunch more.

The key thing to understand: some tools run on shared infrastructure. That's how they keep the price so low. Your work stays private — you just download your content after you create it. And for tools like GPT, you use temporary sessions.

Is it the same as having your own dedicated seat? Not exactly. But for most creators, dropshippers, and marketers who don't need enterprise-level usage caps, it's more than enough.
